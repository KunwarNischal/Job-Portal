Job Description

Labornum is seeking a highly skilled and passionate UI/UX Designer to join our dynamic product team. You will be responsible for creating intuitive, beautiful, and functional user interfaces across our web and mobile platforms. The ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced environment and has a strong portfolio showcasing user-centered design solutions.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with product managers and engineers to define and implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals, and experience.
  • Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to engineering.
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows, and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas.
  • Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback.
  • Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices, and standards.
